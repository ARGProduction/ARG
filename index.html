Our Services

LED
Screens

ARG Production Offers Smart Solutions For Your Multimedia Shows. A Wide Range Of LED Screens, Theater Projectors And Event Projectors. We Also Provide Media Servers, Multi-Link Capture Devices And Scaler/Switchers To Run Your Multi-Screen Shows.

Lighting

Our advanced lighting solutions include powerful beams, vibrant city colors, versatile washers, and cutting-edge fixtures designed to transform any event into a visual masterpiece with stunning illumination and dynamic effects.

Sound
Systems

ARG Production Is Equipped To Handle Any Event Where Professional Audio And Lighting Are Key. ARG Also Offers A Skilled Sales And Distribution Team For All The Top Electronic And Staging Brands Including Electro-Voice, Yamaha, Meyer Sound & MIDAS.

Theatrical
Design

Theatre Design, The Art And Technique Of Designing And Building A Space, A Theatre Intended Primarily For The Performance Of Drama And Its Allied Arts By Live Performers Who Are Physically Present In Front Of A Live Audience.

Content
Creation

Whether It’s Creative Animated Content, Bespoke Creative Project, A Single Screen, A Whole Network Of Screens Or A Pixel Mapping. We Will Create A Powerful Content Solution For Your Production And Budget.

Rigging

ARG Production Has Got A Wide Range Of Prolyte Truss, Prolyte Roof System, Prolyft Chain Hoists And Towers; Because We Believe They Are The Best In The Market. As Well As Being High Quality, They Are Durable, Easy To Assemble And Very User Friendly, Ensuring Our Clients Get Excellent Value And Maximum Flexibility At Affordable Prices.
